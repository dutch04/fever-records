Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 1-2 business days. Delivery time depends on your location: Domestic orders typically take 3-5 business days, while international orders take 7-14 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs fees.

What if my vinyl arrives damaged?

We pack our records with extreme care, but if your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days with photos of the damage. We'll send a replacement at no extra cost.

Products

What's the difference between new & used vinyl?

New vinyl is sealed and unplayed. Used vinyl is pre-owned and graded (Mint, Near Mint, Very Good, Good, Fair) based on condition. All used records are cleaned and tested before shipping.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Currently, we do not offer trade-ins. However, we always buy back rare and valuable records. Contact us for an appraisal.

Are your records authentic?

Absolutely. We guarantee all records are original pressings from official labels. We do not sell reissues or bootlegs unless clearly marked.

Returns & Refunds

What's your return policy?

You may return unopened, new records within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Used records are final sale. Items must be in original condition with shrink wrap intact (if applicable).

How do I initiate a return?

Contact us via the contact page with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide a return shipping label and instructions. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility.
