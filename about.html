About us

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate music enthusiasts, Fever Records started as a small collection shared among friends. The goal was simple: to bring rare and beloved albums back into circulation. As word spread, so did our collection. We began sourcing from estate sales, record fairs, and direct contributions from the community, expanding our catalog to cover diverse genres and eras. Today, Fever Records is a hub for both seasoned collectors and newcomers to the vinyl world. We continue to grow, adding new releases and hidden gems while maintaining our commitment to quality.

Our Mission & Values

Passion for Music

At Fever Records, we believe music is an experience. We curate a selection of vinyl that celebrates the warmth, depth, and artistry that only records can provide.

Quality & Authenticity

We are committed to offering authentic, high-quality vinyl records. Every item is carefully sourced and inspected to ensure you receive the best listening experience.

Community

We foster a community of music lovers, collectors, and enthusiasts. Join us in appreciating the culture and craft surrounding vinyl records.
